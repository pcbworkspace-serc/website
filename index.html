About SERC

Space Engineered Robotics Creations

SERC (Space Engineered Robotics Creations) is dedicated to building innovative PCB-based robotics tools for education and space engineering applications.

Our Mission

We create accessible hardware and software solutions that empower students, engineers, and space enthusiasts to learn, build, and innovate with electronics and PCB design.

What We Build

From educational MiniMEE kits for beginners to advanced SpaceMiniMEE platforms for CubeSats, Mars Rovers, Rockets, and more — SERC provides the tools you need for your mission.
